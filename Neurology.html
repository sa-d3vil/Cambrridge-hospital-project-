Neurology center

The neurologists at Cambridge Hospital diagnose and treat a full range of brain, spinal cord and nerve issues, affecting various areas and functions of the body. Whether you're dealing with dizziness and headaches, a seizure disorder or a cognitive issue like Parkinson's or Alzheimer's, the team at C.H can provide expert testing and treatment.

As a patient, you get focused, personal attention from doctors who take your concerns seriously, get to know you and work with your PCP and other providers to deliver the best possible care.

Services at Their Best

CHA neurologists are experienced in a wide range of neurological needs - from common conditions to the most complex. While they have areas of special expertise, they are all experts in general neurology, allowing them to provide the care your need.

Helping prevent disease is just as important to us as treating it. We look for early signs of cognitive issues like memory loss or dementia, as well as risk factors for stroke and other diseases.

World-class care in your own community

The neurologists at CHA are highly trained. All are board-certified and fellowship-trained, with experience treating patients from around the world, both at CHA and at other hospitals. All of our neurologists are also Harvard Medical School instructors, helping to train the next generation of doctors. Medical students are a vibrant part of the neurology department at CHA, learning from our patients and doctors alike.
