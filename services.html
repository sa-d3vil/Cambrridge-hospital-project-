WELCOME TO CAMBRIDGE HOSPITAL

A state-of-the art modern facility in the heart of the Gujarat state, it is spread over 10 acres and has a built-up area of over 440000 square feet. Currently the hospital has 800+ beds with a capacity to expand to 1000 beds.

Cambridge Hospital, Ahmedabad is a tertiary care flagship unit of the Cambridge Hospitals Group.The Hospital focuses on centres of excellence like Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Orthopaedics including Knee Replacement, Hip Replacement and Spine Surgery, Cancer, Emergency Medicine and Solid Organ Transplants besides the complete range of more than 35 allied medical disciplines under the same roof.
